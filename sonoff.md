## FTDI232 Pinout
* RX - Green
* TX - Red
* VCC - Orange
* CTS - Yellow
* GND - Brown

## Sonfoff Pinout
* 1SQ - VCC (Orange)
* 2 - RX (Red TX)
* 3 - TX (Green RX)
* 4 - GND (Brown)
* 5 - GPIO
